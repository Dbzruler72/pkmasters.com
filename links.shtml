Both a web browser and internet suite, Opera is known for its strict adherence to web standards and innovations now imitated by many popular browsers such as Firefox, Internet Explorer, Safari, and Google Chrome. As well as web browsing, Opera can send and receive e-mail via its built-in e-mail client, use IRC, download via BitTorrent, read web feeds, and allow almost full customization of its interface. Despite its speed and rife and diverse functionality, Opera continues to hold less than three percent of total desktop browser market share.
